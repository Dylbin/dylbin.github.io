NineAnimator

Compatibility

iOS 11, 12, 13, 14, 15

Description

A simple yet elegant way of watching anime on your favorite websites without ads.

Features

  • Ad-Free and no logins.
  • Super Clean UI + Dark Mode.
  • Get notifications when new episodes come out.
  • Apple's native video playback interface.
  • Picture in Picture playback on iOS/iPadOS 14+ devices.
  • Playback History & Auto Resume.
  • Support for Multiple Anime Websites.
  • Integration with HomeKit
  • Discord Rich Presence Integration (macOS Only).
  • Handoff & Siri Shortcuts.
  • Download & Play Episodes Offline.
  • Third Party Anime Listing & Tracking Websites (View & Edit).
